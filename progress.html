Background

The flow of students through courses contained within major is of interest to both students and educators. Students may wish to know common course sequences taken by their predecessors: are there many ways to complete the major, or are there paths that lead to better outcomes? Educators meanwhile may seek to understand the level of preparation students have reached as they progress to courses later in the curriculum: are students using introductory course locally, or transferring them in? Are they skipping recommended courses?

Analysis

Sankey diagrams and sunburst diagrams both give general overviews of this flow. Using PLA-MOOC data, we demonstrate this with a sunburst diagram.
